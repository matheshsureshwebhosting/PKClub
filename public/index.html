About Us

PK Donatary Club is a volunteers based service, we help you as much as possible throughout Tamilnadu. We help and guide many COVID -19 patients and their family members with Testing Assistance, Bed availability, Oxygen assistance, Ambulance assistance, Covid patient's pet care, Covid medicine and government helpline Numbers.

Note : We are not Commercial / Dealers. We are working with so many volunteers to bring the details Which will help you at the right time.
